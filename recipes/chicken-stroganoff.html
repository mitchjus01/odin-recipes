Healthier Slow Cooker Chicken Stroganoff

Chicken breast cooked in the slow cooker with a creamy sauce mixture.

Includes peas, low-fat cream cheese, butter, and cream of chicken soup.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon chopped carrot
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 ounces Neufchatel cheese
  • 8 ounces cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup frozen peas

Steps

  1. Combine the carrot, parsley, onion, garlic, lemon zest, salt, and pepper in a food processor. Process until finely chopped and transfer to slow cooker. Add chicken and butter and mix. Cook on Low for 5 to 6 hours.
  2. Stir in Neufchatel cheese, cream of chicken soup, and peas. Cook on High until heated through, about 30 minutes.
